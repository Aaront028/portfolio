About me

What I love about programming is being able to problem solve and work within a team or pair program to reach an end goal. I enjoy sharing ideas and opinions about problems, building relations with work mates, and I especially love the rewarding feeling after solving a particularly tough problem.

For the past 15 years, I have continuously found myself drawn to technology. I have since built websites with various platforms like Squarespace, Wix, and Weebly for my photography portfolio, and other businesses. A couple of years ago I dove deeper into how to build apps and websites from scratch, and self taught on multiple online courses. I have also graduated as a fullstack web developer.

My stack includes: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, Handlebars, Redux and React.js. I am excited to learn more frameworks and languages to further strengthen my skills in web-development.

I had experience starting and running my own sushi business, where I developed time management, communication, and interpersonal skills. I work well under pressure and have developed a good rapport with my clients and staff. Being a business owner has also made me a very detail oriented person and commercially aware. I am confident that my abilities to create relationships and work in a team, combined with my eye for detail and commercial awareness will be an asset.
